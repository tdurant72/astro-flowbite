# Case Study: The $9M Regional Engine

## Transforming NCGTPEDR by Architecting a Regional Identity.

### Executive Summary
**Sublime UX** executed a strategic redesign for the **North Carolina Global TransPark Economic Development Region (NCGTPEDR)**. By implementing the **Revenue Engine Architect** protocol, we transformed a visually cluttered legacy site into a high-conversion platform, revealing an estimated **$9,000,000 in Optimized Opportunity Gap** annually.


### Phase 1: The Revenue Audit (Identifying the "Conversion Leak")
Before the redesign, the site suffered from **Invisible Friction**, failing the "Site Selector’s 5-Second Test." Our technical audit identified three primary pillars of failure:

* **The Identity Gap:** The primary headline was geographic ("The Heart of NC") rather than **Intent-Driven**, failing to capture industrial interest immediately.
* **High Cognitive Load:** A "Red-on-Red" UI and broken internal links created a **Credibility Gap** for multi-million dollar industrial projects.
* **The Cost of Inaction:** Based on ~500 monthly visitors and a $1M project baseline, the original site was leaking **$6,000,000 in uncaptured regional investment** annually.

![Before](/images/projects/ncgtpedr-project/before.webp)

### The Site Selector’s 5-Second Test: FAIL
Headline was geographic ("The Heart of NC"), not benefit-driven.

### Friction Audit: HIGH
No primary "Connect" button in the header. Site selectors had to dig for data.

### Authority Stack: WEAK
Trust signals (Partner logos) were buried in the footer, losing credibility.


### Phase 2: Technical Implementation (The "After" State)
We deployed a three-tier architecture optimized for **Institutional Authority** and **User Intent Matching**.

#### 1. Above-the-Fold Intent Matching
We replaced the generic "Heart of NC" headline with: **"Scale Globally with North Carolina’s Industrial Engine."**
* **Technical Result:** In 3 seconds, a CEO identifies the **Service Capability** (Industrial Scale) and the **Value Proposition** (Global Competition).

#### 2. The Logistics Authority Stack (Data as a Weapon)
We transitioned from static lists to a **High-Density Industrial Dashboard** to solve the #1 concern for industrialists: **Talent Availability.**
* **Key Data Point:** 1.2M+ population within a 45-minute commute.
* **Strategic Impact:** Proactively addresses logistics and workforce supply chain concerns.

#### 3. Zero-Friction Conversion Path
* **Sticky CTA:** A persistent "Connect with a Strategist" button ensures the path to a high-value meeting is always one click away.
* **AI Mission Control:** An authoritative chatbot provides instant data-driven answers, reducing bounce rates for technical queries.
